Main goal of this mod is to make Music Discs craftable, which is especially helpful for those, who love to use Jukebox. It also supports many popular mods!

Do you want your mod to be compatible?

- For this you can add compatibility to your mod, but please indicate it on your page with a link to this mod.

- You can also contact the author, he can be found in the discord-server (link above). And already in the next update your mod to get compatibility with this mod.

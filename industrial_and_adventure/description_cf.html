Revival of the old modpack! But on a new version and with a bunch of new mods! Explore the world with the new generation, do winemaking, create your own plant farms, visit a bunch of worlds, demolish all the dungeons that will meet you on the way, well, and deal with the industrialism itself, build a factory from a bunch of mechanisms that will be both on energy and on steam, create a ME-network and make autocrafts to it. And that's not all you can do in this modpack, but it's all up to you. I wish you good luck in going through this modpack!

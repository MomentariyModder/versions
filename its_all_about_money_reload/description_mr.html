This is another money mod for Minecraft. The mod adds: Dollar bills and their stacks, powerful tools, new bullion and menirals, food and ore.

To create a 1 dollar bill you will need: thread, paper, wool and gold. Once you have gotten the resources you need, use them to make Cotton Sheet and Linen Sheet. Then combine all the sheets with the gold to make a 1 dollar bill.

To create other dollar bills, use the bills themselves, for example you can make a 2 dollar bill from 2 1-dollar bills.
